Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here at Adventures on the Rapids is to help people have fun on the water. Its as simple as that. We want everyone to be safe, and have fun, and having a website like this one to turn to can make all that so much easier.

Audience

Hi there! We know as a parent it can be tough to find family friendly activities, that are fun for both adults and children. We here at Adventures on the Rapids(Subsidiary of the Dry Oar Company) have a solution! Do you want to have a great time on the water, but don't know where to start, or how to find the time? Then Adventures on the Rapids is for you! To make it easy, simple and cheap for people to have once-in-a-lifetime experiences, we've assembled all the information you'll need for your adventure to begin. Here you'll find information on white water rafting, safety measures, and AOTR(Adventures On The Rapids)locations near you. Being a member of this site can also win you and your family exclusive deals on rafting trips, along with anual and semi-anual passes. This site can be accessed anywhere from your laptop to your phone, so there's no need to worry that you won't have access in any situation you may be in.
