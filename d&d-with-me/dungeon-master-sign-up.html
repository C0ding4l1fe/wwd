Dungeon Master Sign Up

 Welcome future or current Dungeon Masters! This page will let you create a personal profile, and submit it to the site for others to view in the Hiring section. This can include your name, experience level (on a scale of beginner, proficient, expert and master), your preferred number of players, and lastly, what sort of game you will be running, be it homebrew, or premade, along with a sort of content blurb to grab players attention as to what sort of story your's will be, and whether or not they'd like to be a part of it.
